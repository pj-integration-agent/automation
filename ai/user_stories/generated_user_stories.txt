Based on the information provided in the image, here are some User Stories for the ParaBank website:

US01: As a customer, I want to be able to log in to my account securely using my username and password, so that I can access my banking services.
- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The login page must have fields for username and password.
  - The login process must authenticate the user's credentials and grant access to the account.
  - Error messages must be displayed if the login fails.
  - The login process must be secure, with measures in place to prevent unauthorized access.

US02: As a customer, I want to be able to withdraw funds from my account using the ATM services, so that I can access my money whenever I need it.
- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The ATM services section must allow customers to withdraw funds from their accounts.
  - The withdrawal process must deduct the correct amount from the customer's account balance.
  - The ATM services must be available 24/7 for customer convenience.
  - The ATM services must provide clear instructions and feedback to the customer during the withdrawal process.

US03: As a customer, I want to be able to transfer funds between my accounts using the online services, so that I can manage my finances efficiently.
- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The online services section must allow customers to transfer funds between their accounts.
  - The transfer process must update the balances of the source and destination accounts correctly.
  - The online services must provide a history of all account transactions, including fund transfers.
  - The online services must be secure and easy to use, with clear instructions and feedback provided to the customer.

US04: As a customer, I want to be able to view my account balances using the online services, so that I can stay informed about my financial status.
- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The online services section must display the current balances of the customer's accounts.
  - The account balance information must be up-to-date and accurate.
  - The account balance information must be easily accessible and intuitive for the customer to understand.
  - The online services must provide clear instructions and guidance on how to view account balances.
